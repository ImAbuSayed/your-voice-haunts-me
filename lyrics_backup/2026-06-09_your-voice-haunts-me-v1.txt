[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the fire when you whisper close
A gentle flame that only midnight knows
Your breath against my skin is all I need
A quiet storm that brings me to my knees

[Verse 1]
We left the crowded rooms and noise behind
Just two shadows that the moonlight finds
You trace a line across my weary chest
And in your touch I finally find my rest

[Pre-Chorus]
The temperature is rising in the dark
A slow burn starting from a single spark
I pull you closer till the shadows blend
I never want this heavy night to end

[Chorus]
Your voice haunts me in the sweetest way
An echo that will never fade away
I want to lose myself inside your eyes
And let the morning take us by surprise

[Post-Chorus]
(Haunts me now, haunts me now)
[Whispered] (I am yours to take)

[Verse 2]
The wooden floors are creaking with our sway
We let the silent moments softly play
My hands are tangled in your messy hair
There is a quiet magic in the air

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The temperature is rising in the dark
A slow burn starting from a single spark
I pull you closer till the shadows blend
I never want this heavy night to end

[Chorus]
Your voice haunts me in the sweetest way
An echo that will never fade away
I want to lose myself inside your eyes
And let the morning take us by surprise

[Post-Chorus 2]
(Haunts me now, haunts me now)
[Whispered] (I am yours to take)

[Bridge]
I feel the fire when you whisper close
A gentle flame that only midnight knows
You pull me closer with a heavy thread
And every word of yours gets in my head
I never thought that I would just concede
There is no running for a heart like Saeed

[Spoken]
(Just look at me right now)
(Let the world fade out while we stay right here)

[Final Chorus]
Your voice haunts me in the sweetest way
An echo that will never fade away
I want to lose myself inside your eyes
And let the morning take us by surprise
